ALH perfumes are available in several sizes to suit your needs. Whether you're looking for a travel-sized option or a statement piece for your vanity, we have the perfect size for you.

How to Choose Your Size

First-Time Buyers

Try our 30ml travel size to test the fragrance before committing to a larger bottle.

Daily Use

Our 100ml size is perfect for regular use at home or work, offering the best value.

Gift Giving

The 200ml statement size makes an impressive gift for special occasions.

Care & Storage

Proper Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep the bottle tightly closed when not in use
  • Avoid extreme temperatures
  • Store upright to prevent leakage

Maximizing Longevity

  • Apply to pulse points for longer-lasting fragrance
  • Layer with matching body lotion or oil
  • Use sparingly - a little goes a long way
  • Replace nozzle if it becomes clogged
