Introduction

Welcome to ALH Luxury Perfume. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and purchase our products.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the site, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, when you participate in activities on the site, or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Personal and contact information such as name, email address, phone number, and shipping address
  • Payment information such as credit card number, and billing address
  • Information related to your purchases, such as items purchased and order history
  • Feedback and correspondence such as your comments and reviews

We also collect information automatically when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Information on how you use our website
  • Location information (with your consent where required by law)

How We Use auto Information

We use the information we collect for various business purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website and services
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website and servicesDevelop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your requests and providing customer support
  • Process your payments and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Send you marketing and promotional communications (with your consent where required)
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and interests

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • For Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information where we are required to do so by law
  • Business Transfers: We may share or transfer your information in connection with a business deal or merger
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent or at your direction

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to object to or restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our website and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
